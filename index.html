Some of the most popular tourist places in Kerala include: Kochi (Cochin) for its historic port city charm and Chinese fishing nets, Alleppey (Alappuzha) renowned for its backwaters and houseboat cruises, Munnar with its sprawling tea plantations and waterfalls, Wayanad a lush green hill station, Varkala a tranquil beach town, Kovalam for scenic beaches, Thekkady a lush green paradise, Kumarakom for backwater experiences, and Thrissur considered the cultural capital of Kerala.
